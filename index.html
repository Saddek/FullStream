          Welcome to FullStream

          A better way to watch Twitch


          About

          FullStream is with the intention made to make it better to watch twitch.tv in a fullscreen interface while still having the ability to chat, view your following channels, having a status bar, Picture in Picture, an automatic channel switcher, support for VODs and more.


          Channel list

          The channels list displays channels you are following on Twitch.

          • Change channel.
          • Activate Picture in Picture.
          • Favorite Channel.
          • Activate game list.
          • Add channel to switcher.
          • Remove the channel.

          Switcher

          The switcher will automatically switch the video and chat embeds to the top channel on the list that is currently live, the list can be sorted by dragging and dropping the channels


          Hotkeys

          Various hotkeys to trigger events

          • S = Toggle sidebar
          • T = Toggle topbar
          • C = Change menu item direction left
          • V = Change menu item direction right
          • B = Switch to previous live channel
          • N = Switch to next live channel
