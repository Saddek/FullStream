          Welcome to FullStream, an alternative way to watch Twitch.tv streams on the web.


          About

          FullStream was initially made to make it easy to watch twitch.tv streams in fullscreen while still having the ability to chat, in addition there has been added some cool features like a channels list, a status bar, an automatic channel switcher, support for twitch VODs and more.


          The channel list

          The channels list displays channels you are following on Twitch, if you hover over a channel you you get more info about it and a few options.


          • The play button let's you tune into the channel by switching the video and chat embed source.

          • Picture in Picture, let's you watch a second stream in a chosen corner.

          • Adds the selected channel to the favorites list. *

          • Creates a list of channels streaming the same game. *

          • Let's you add the channel to the switcher. *

          • Removes the channel from the channels list. **
          • * Only Twitch channels.
            ** Only non-Twitch channels and Switcher channels.

          The Switcher

          The switcher will automatically switch the video and chat embeds to the top channel on the list that is currently live, the list can be sorted by dragging and dropping the channels


          Hotkeys

          Various hotkeys to trigger events

          • S = Toggle sidebar
          • T = Toggle topbar
          • C = Change menu item direction left
          • V = Change menu item direction right
          • B = Switch to previous live channel
          • N = Switch to next live channel
